About Sharma Traders

Our Journey

Built on Trust, Grown on Quality

Sharma Traders was founded in 2010 by Mr. Ramesh Sharma with a single vision: to make high-quality wholesale commodities accessible to every business — from corner shops to large distributors.

Starting with a small warehouse and a handful of loyal clients, we have expanded over 15 years into a full-scale operation with over 1,000 product lines, a 50-member team, and a logistics network spanning all major Indian cities.

Today, over 500 happy clients — retailers, hotels, restaurants, and FMCG distributors — place their trust in us month after month.

🔭 Our Vision
